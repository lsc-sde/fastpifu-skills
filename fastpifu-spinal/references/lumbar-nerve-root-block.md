# Lumbar nerve root block (epidural)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case lumbar nerve-root block, often directly listed from a virtual MDT; no clinical tabletop review required.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- 6-month PIFU (communicated at the start of the pathway); no clinical tabletop review required.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Diagnostic response but pain returns — consider surgery (do not repeat the injection).

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- At end of the 6-month PIFU period if no further intervention needed.

## Surveillance / review interval

6-month PIFU.
