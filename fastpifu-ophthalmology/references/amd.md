# Age-related macular degeneration (AMD)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Dry and wet AMD; wet AMD is an injection pathway with high-volume virtual review.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway — managed via virtual review and strict treat-and-extend, then discharge with self-monitoring.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Wet AMD on treatment — virtual review every interval (treat-and-extend); first injection within 14 days of detection/referral.
- Suspected wet AMD — urgent assessment within one working day.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Inactive treated disease — monitor 3-monthly for two years for reactivation, then discharge with self-monitoring/red-flag advice.
- Permanent structural damage / no further treatment — discharge to routine sight test with ECLO/low-vision support.
- Dry AMD — no referral; regular sight test with diet/lifestyle/monitoring advice.

## Surveillance / review interval

Treat-and-extend on treatment; post-discharge self-monitoring with red-flag advice.
