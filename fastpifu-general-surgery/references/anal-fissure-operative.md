# Anal fissure (operative treatment)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case operative anal fissure treatment (Botox, lateral sphincterotomy, advancement flap).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU for three months (after Botox injections — no face-to-face follow-up).

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Face-to-face appointment at 6–8 weeks after lateral anal sphincterotomy or advancement flap.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Uncomplicated Botox — discharge to PIFU.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU for 3 months.
