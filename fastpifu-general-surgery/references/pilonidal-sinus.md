# Pilonidal sinus surgery

## Scope

Day-case pilonidal sinus surgery.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU for three months.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Flap procedures or open wounds — face-to-face appointment at 6–8 weeks.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Excision and primary closure of a simple sinus — no face-to-face follow-up; discharge to PIFU.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU for 3 months.
