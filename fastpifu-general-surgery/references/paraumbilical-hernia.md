# Paraumbilical hernia repair

## Scope

Elective day-case paraumbilical hernia repair.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not offered — discharge with no PIFU.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- High risk of complications — targeted face-to-face appointment at 6–8 weeks.
- Early wound problem (access via day surgery).

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Uncomplicated small primary hernia — no routine follow-up; discharge. Enrol in the BHS hernia registry.
