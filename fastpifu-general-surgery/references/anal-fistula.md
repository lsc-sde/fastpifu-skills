# Anal fistula

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case anal fistula surgery; up-to-date MRI for complex/recurrent disease.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU for three months (after lay-open of a low simple fistula).

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Face-to-face appointment at 6–8 weeks after lay-open of a complex fistula, seton insertion, or sphincter-preserving techniques.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Lay-open of low simple fistula — discharge to PIFU.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU for 3 months.
