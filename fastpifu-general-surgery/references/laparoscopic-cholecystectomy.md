# Laparoscopic cholecystectomy

## Scope

Elective day-case laparoscopic cholecystectomy.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU for six months for recurrent symptoms, jaundice or diarrhoea.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Targeted follow-up for dysplasia or carcinoma found in the specimen.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Uncomplicated — no routine follow-up; specialist-nurse phone line for early advice.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU for 6 months.
