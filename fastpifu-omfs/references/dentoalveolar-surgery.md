# Dentoalveolar surgery (incl. MRONJ-risk and soft-tissue procedures)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Simple dentoalveolar surgery (extractions, surgical removals, apical surgery, exposures), soft-tissue procedures, and the higher-risk MRONJ subgroup.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Salivary duct manipulation ± stone removal — PIFU pathway.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Irradiated patients, or patients on bisphosphonates/antiresorptive/anti-angiogenic drugs — follow-up at 8 weeks per SDCEP 'Oral health management of patients at risk of MRONJ'.
- Patients on orthodontic pathways — appropriate orthodontic review.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Routine simple dentoalveolar surgery — no surgical follow-up required.
- Soft-tissue procedures (polyps, mucoceles) — telephone/letter with histology, no follow-up.
