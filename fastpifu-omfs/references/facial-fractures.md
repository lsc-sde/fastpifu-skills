# Facial fractures (mandibular, condyle, zygomatic, orbital)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Open reduction/fixation or closed reduction of facial fractures. Excludes children with developing dentition / growing condyles, and late orbital problems (e.g. enophthalmos).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Orbital fractures — discharge to PIFU after the early review and orthoptic follow-up.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Mandibular fractures: follow-up at 10–14 days and final follow-up at 8–12 weeks.
- Orbital fractures: follow-up at 10–14 days plus orthoptic-team follow-up per unit protocol.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Zygomatic fractures: follow-up at 10–14 days then discharge.

## Surveillance / review interval

Early review 10–14 days; mandibular final review 8–12 weeks.
