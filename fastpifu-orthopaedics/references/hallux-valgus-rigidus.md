# Hallux valgus/rigidus (bunion) surgery

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case bunion surgery after community non-surgical management.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU option available only if the patient is not meeting expected milestones.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Dressing clinic at 1–2 weeks (nurse-led, surgeon available); 6-week post-surgery appointment with follow-up X-ray only if needed.
- Patient not meeting expected milestones.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Meeting expected milestones at 6 weeks — discharge.

## Surveillance / review interval

1–2 weeks dressing clinic; 6-week review.
