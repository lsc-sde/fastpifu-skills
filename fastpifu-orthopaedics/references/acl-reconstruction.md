# Anterior cruciate ligament reconstruction

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Uncomplicated ACL reconstruction (the ~75% standard-recovery majority).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU with access to therapy-led provision for 12 months post-op.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- One appointment (virtual/F2F) at 6 weeks–3 months and a 9-month follow-up with the surgical team.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Recovered and meeting milestones at end of the pathway.

## Surveillance / review interval

Reviews at 6 weeks–3 months and 9 months; PIFU 12 months.
