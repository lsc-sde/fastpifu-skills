# Posterior lumbar decompression / discectomy

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Posterior lumbar decompression or discectomy (2nd priority after injections).
Benchmark: 2 appointments pre-procedure and 1 post-procedure.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- 6-month PIFU duration (communicated at the beginning).

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Wound review in primary care at 2 weeks; 2–3-week post-op virtual physio follow-up; 6–8-week virtual post-op follow-up with the surgical team.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- At end of the 6-month PIFU period once recovered.

## Surveillance / review interval

Wound 2 wk; physio 2–3 wk; surgical 6–8 wk; PIFU 6 months.
