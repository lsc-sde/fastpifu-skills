## Scope

Most acne is managed in primary care per NICE; secondary care for specified indications.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Generally not a PIFU pathway — see isotretinoin file if relevant.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Severe acne or under specialist treatment requiring review.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Not severe or unsuitable for isotretinoin — discharge with advice per NICE; consider spironolactone via primary care/A&G.
