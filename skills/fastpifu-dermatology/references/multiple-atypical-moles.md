# Multiple atypical moles

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Usually education and discharge with self-surveillance, per local policy.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Reliable self-surveillance with access back if concerns.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Very high risk (strong personal or family history of melanoma) — scheduled follow-up.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Suspected cancer excluded — educate and discharge with self-surveillance.
