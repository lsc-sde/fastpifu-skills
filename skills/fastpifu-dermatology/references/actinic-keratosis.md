# Actinic keratosis (AK)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

AK is usually treated and discharged at first secondary-care appointment (BAD 2017).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- High risk of cancer — consider PIFU post-treatment with self-surveillance education.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Very high risk (e.g. transplant recipient) — scheduled follow-up.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Treat on first appointment and discharge with prevention/skin-surveillance information (the default for most AK).
