## Scope

Isotretinoin course follow-up, governed by the MHRA pregnancy-prevention programme.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway during the course.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Follow-up per pregnancy-prevention programme for those of childbearing potential; one F2F appointment mandated by MHRA, others may be virtual. New MHRA rules allow less-than-monthly review for some very-low-pregnancy-risk patients.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Course complete and criteria met — discharge.
