## Scope

Day-case functional endoscopic sinus surgery for chronic rhinosinusitis after a ≥3-month trial of medical therapy.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- After the first post-operative face-to-face follow-up at 3–6 weeks — PIFU available for one year.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Within the first post-operative review window (first follow-up at 3–6 weeks).
- Ongoing surgical concern at first review.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Recovered with no concerns at the end of the PIFU year.

## Surveillance / review interval

First follow-up 3–6 weeks post-op; PIFU available for 1 year thereafter.
