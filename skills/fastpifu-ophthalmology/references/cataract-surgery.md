# Cataract surgery

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Routine and complex cataract surgery; most routine patients are discharged to optometry.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway — routine patients discharge to community optometry with urgent/CUES return route.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Complex patient or serious/unstable ocular co-morbidity — optometrist-commissioned follow-up or HCP/consultant-led hospital appointment.
- Minor complication or IOP check — ±1–2 day hospital visit.
- Fellow-eye surgery requiring F2F per ocular complexity.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Routine patient — discharge to sight test with optometry data return; urgent/CUES pathway if needed.
