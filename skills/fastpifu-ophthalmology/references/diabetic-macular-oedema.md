# Diabetic macular oedema (DMO)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

DMO identified at sight test or DESP; asynchronous/virtual review with injection where indicated.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway — virtual/asynchronous review and treat-and-extend, then discharge back to DESP when stable.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- CRT >400 µm — offered IVT; strict treat-and-extend with certainty of appointments.
- PDR/R3a — appointment within six weeks, must be face-to-face.
- DESP-referred DMO — appointment at HES within 13 weeks (may be virtual).

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Stable maculopathy and retinopathy — discharge back to DESP; inform GP and optometrist with ECLO/low-vision support if needed.

## Surveillance / review interval

Six-monthly DAVRAP retinal monitoring (individualised) while in service.
