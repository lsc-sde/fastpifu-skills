## Scope

Glaucoma and ocular hypertension via single-point-of-access triage and a Diagnostic Asynchronous Virtual Review Assessment Pathway (DAVRAP). Intervals and discharge per GIRFT Best Practice for Glaucoma Services.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway in the conventional sense — virtual review at risk-stratified intervals; face-to-face capacity focused on higher-risk patients.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Use the follow-up intervals defined in GIRFT Best Practice for Glaucoma Services (e.g. OHT/suspect COAG/COAG by conversion, IOP control and RAG risk).
- Patients receiving laser/surgical intervention as indicated by virtual review.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Use the discharge criteria defined in GIRFT Best Practice for Glaucoma Services (e.g. new patients with no OHT/no glaucoma 'disease' — discharge after first attendance with A&G letter and recommend regular community sight test; treated PACS-plus after LPI or cataract surgery).

## Surveillance / review interval

Per GIRFT Best Practice for Glaucoma Services follow-up interval tables (months by conversion/IOP control/RAG risk).
