# Temporomandibular joint problems and myofascial pain

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

TMJ problems (myogenous/arthrogenous) and myofascial pain, including Botox for those who have failed self-supported management.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Botox for myofascial pain after failed self-management — PIFU pathway (Botox given at first follow-up).

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- TMJ problems — follow the Faster Diagnostic Standard (FDS) guidance for the relevant diagnosis.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Managed with advice and self-supported management succeeding.
