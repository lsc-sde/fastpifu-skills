# Orthognathic osteotomy (single-jaw and bimaxillary)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Sagittal-split / vertical-ramus / Le Fort I osteotomy on the orthognathic pathway.
Excludes osteotomies requiring post-operative intermaxillary fixation.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not a PIFU pathway — handed to the orthodontic team.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Follow-up at 1 week post-operatively.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- After the 1-week review — discharge to the orthodontic team.

## Surveillance / review interval

1-week post-op review.
