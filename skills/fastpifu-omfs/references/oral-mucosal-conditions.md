# Oral mucosal conditions (dry mouth, geographic tongue, recurrent oral ulceration, oral lichen planus)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Non-surgical oral-mucosal conditions: dry mouth, geographic tongue (erythema migrans), recurrent oral ulceration, and oral lichen planus (reticular and erosive).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Erosive oral lichen planus (no dysplasia) — once treatment is established, PIFU is suitable.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Erosive OLP while treatment is being established (treatments vary from advice to systemics).
- Diagnostic uncertainty requiring biopsy/bloods.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Dry mouth, geographic tongue, recurrent oral ulceration, reticular oral lichen planus — history/clinical appearance (±tests), give advice, no follow-up.
