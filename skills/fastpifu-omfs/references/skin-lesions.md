# Head and neck skin lesions (OMFS)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Skin lesions of the head and neck (melanoma, cSCC, BCC, AK, SCC in situ).
Excludes major head-and-neck cancer.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Follow the GIRFT/BAD Dermatology follow-up protocols — see the dermatology skill files for the per-lesion disposition (PSFU/PIFU where self-monitoring is reliable).

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Per dermatology risk-category schedules.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Per dermatology pathways (e.g. low-risk cSCC, single BCC).
