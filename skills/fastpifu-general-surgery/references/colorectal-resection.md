# Colorectal resection

## Scope

Inpatient elective colorectal resection (not the cancer-surgery referral pathway).
Disposition depends on pathology.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- No PIFU; all patients have ward/CNS access for early wound or stoma problems.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- All patients: 6–8 week face-to-face follow-up.
- Colorectal cancer: active surveillance for 5 years with risk-stratified CT and CEA arranged remotely.
- Ulcerative colitis: keep under review.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Crohn's: discharge and transfer to gastroenterology.
- Diverticular disease: discharge.

## Surveillance / review interval

6–8 week review for all; cancer surveillance 5 years.
