# Dupuytren's disease

## Scope

Percutaneous fasciotomy or day-case surgery for Dupuytren's.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU (12 months) for patients not ready for or requiring surgery.
- PIFU (6–12 weeks) for non-complex post-operative cases.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Skin graft / complex cases — face-to-face follow-up with the consultant.
- Virtual review by surgeon/ESP where suitable (outpatient cases).

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Simple case recovered after virtual review.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU 12 months (pre-surgery) or 6–12 weeks (post-op non-complex).
