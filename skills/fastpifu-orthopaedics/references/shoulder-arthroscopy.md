# Shoulder arthroscopy (rotator cuff repair / subacromial decompression)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case shoulder arthroscopy.
Recovery milestones gate PIFU.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Subacromial decompression: PIFU at 6 weeks (after the 1st follow-up) unless not recovering as expected.
- Rotator cuff repair: PIFU at 6 months (after the 2nd follow-up) unless not recovering as expected.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Subacromial decompression: 1st appointment at 6–8 weeks post-op.
- Rotator cuff repair: 1st at 6–8 weeks and 2nd at 3–6 months post-op; 3rd at 12 months if not recovering as expected.
- Not recovering as expected.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Recovered and meeting milestones at the relevant PIFU point.

## Surveillance / review interval

SAD: 1st at 6–8 wk then PIFU.
RCR: 6–8 wk and 3–6 mo then PIFU.
