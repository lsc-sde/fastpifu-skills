# Ganglion

## Scope

Day-case ganglion aspiration/excision; many aspirated and discharged same day.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- PIFU — 3 months for simple aspirations and 3 months for surgical cases.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Wound therapy/review only if patient/surgical team are concerned.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Nurse-led discharge; sutures (if any) removed in primary care.

## Surveillance / review interval

PIFU 3 months.
