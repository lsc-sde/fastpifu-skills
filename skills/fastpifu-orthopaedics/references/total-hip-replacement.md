# Elective primary total hip replacement

## Scope

Uncomplicated elective primary THR (the ~85% standard-recovery majority).

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Safety-netting and PIFU for 1 year after the single post-operative review.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- One appointment (virtual or F2F) at 6 weeks–3 months post-surgery with the surgical team (CNS/ACP/physio/SCP).
  No repeat X-ray.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Recovered and meeting milestones at end of PIFU year.

## Surveillance / review interval

One review at 6 weeks–3 months; PIFU for 1 year.
