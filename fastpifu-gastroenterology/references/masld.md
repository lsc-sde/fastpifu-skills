# MASLD (metabolic dysfunction-associated steatotic liver disease)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Fatty liver referred for fibrosis evaluation; disposition driven by fibrosis score.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Intermediate fibrosis score — may be suitable for PIFU until a repeat fibrosis assessment is performed.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Proven cirrhosis — stable-cirrhosis surveillance pathway.
- Intermediate fibrosis needing repeat assessment in 1–2 years.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Low fibrosis score — discharge back to primary care, with possible repeat fibrosis assessment in ~3 years (sooner if ongoing risk factors).
- No/minimal fibrosis — discharge with advice on future re-assessment.

## Data that must be present to assess

| Required data point | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| Fibrosis score (e.g. FibroScan/ELF) | Determines disposition |
| Ongoing metabolic risk factors | Set re-assessment interval |

## Surveillance / review interval

Repeat fibrosis assessment 1–2 years (intermediate) or ~3 years (low/ongoing risk).
