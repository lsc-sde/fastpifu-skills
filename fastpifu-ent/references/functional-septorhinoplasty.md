# Functional septorhinoplasty

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Day-case functional septorhinoplasty for nasal obstruction after a 3-month trial of medical therapy.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not routinely offered — discharge if no concerns.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- First follow-up at 1–2 weeks if nasal splints used; second follow-up at 6 weeks–3 months.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- No concerns at the second follow-up (6 weeks–3 months) — discharge, no routine PIFU.

## Surveillance / review interval

1–2 weeks (if splints) then 6 weeks–3 months; no routine PIFU.
