# Paediatric otitis media with effusion (OME)

<!-- concept_ids: (completed by data team) -->
<!-- author: derived from GIRFT OP guide module 2 | reviewed_by: <lead> | last_updated: 2026-06 -->

## Scope

Children with ≥3-month hearing difficulty, grommets (±adenoidectomy) where hearing aids declined/unsuitable. Paediatric pathway.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Discharge with PIFU after the six-week hearing test, provided hearing has improved.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Face-to-face follow-up with hearing test at 6 weeks post-surgery (default).
- Hearing NOT improved at the six-week test.
- Concerning social factors or neurodevelopmental conditions.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Nurse-led discharge if BADS criteria met and recovery uncomplicated.

## Surveillance / review interval

Hearing test at 6 weeks post-surgery; PIFU thereafter if hearing improved.
