# Post-skin-lesion surgery

## Scope

Follow-up after excision of benign lesions/BCCs.

## PIFU — suitable for patient-initiated follow-up

- Not routinely required for benign lesions/BCCs.

## RETAIN_FOLLOW_UP — timed specialist follow-up still needed

- Targeted follow-up only for dysplasia/carcinoma on histology or wound concern.

## DISCHARGE — no ongoing specialist follow-up needed

- Benign lesion/BCC excised — write with results and information, no follow-up.
